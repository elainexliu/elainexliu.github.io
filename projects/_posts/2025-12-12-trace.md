## TRACE FPGAinting: Three-Dimensional Rendering with an Augmented Camera Environment

Worked with Tatiana to build a fun little project--we present an FPGA implementation of threedimensional drawing and rendering of an object path. Our goal is to create a system that transfers occurrences in the physical world to the virtual world. This hardware networking stack interfaces with a 25 fps OV5640 camera and an HDMI 1280x720 monitor. We implement this design using custom object-capture, interpolation, and rendering modules on the Xilinx Spartan-7 XC7S50 chip, evaluate its performance and quality, and discuss potential areas for future expansion and improvement.
