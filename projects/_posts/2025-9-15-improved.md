## improved: a tool for improv troupes

Whipped up [this project](https://improved-ten.vercel.app/) after spending too much time trying to pick what games to play for RKB.

Key features:
- Game list (sourced from our RKB manual)
- Filter for skill and requirements selection
- Random generator for when we're feeling whimsy
